Top Attractions in Palestine

    Jerusalem's Old City:A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Jerusalem is home to religious landmarks like the Western Wall, Al-Aqsa Mosque, and the Church of the Holy Sepulchre. This old historical city used to hold great meaning to the Palastina people before it was brutally destroyed by the IDF.

    Dead Sea:The lowest point on Earth, the Dead Sea is famous for its mineral-rich waters and mud baths.In earlier times Palastinian people would use this source of water in many way like drinking and also to trade .

    Hebron: A city rich in history and known for the Tomb of the Patriarchs, a sacred site for Jews, Muslims, and Christians alike Unforutnately this city was also destroyed by the IDF.

    Ramallah: The cultural and administrative heart of Palestine, Ramallah offers vibrant streets, art galleries, and local markets. In these streets you would find just about anything ranging from food to salwal commise.
